Classic Margherita Pizza

This Classic Margherita Pizza embodies the simplicity and deliciousness of Italian cuisine, focusing on fresh, high-quality ingredients. A thin, crispy crust serves as the base, topped with a rich tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella cheese, and aromatic basil leaves, then drizzled with extra virgin olive oil. It's a timeless dish that brings the essence of Italy right to your table, perfect for any occasion.

The secret to its mouthwatering flavor lies in the balance of its components, from the tangy sweetness of the tomato sauce to the creamy texture of the mozzarella, all enhanced by the basil's freshness. Baked to perfection, the edges are slightly charred, giving it a rustic appeal that's hard to resist. Follow this recipe to create an authentic Margherita pizza that's sure to impress your friends and family.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball of pizza dough (about 12 inches in diameter)
  • 1/2 cup tomato sauce (preferably homemade or high-quality store-bought)
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to its highest setting, typically between 475째F (245째C) and 500째F (260째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven at this time.
  2. On a floured surface, stretch the pizza dough out to about 12 inches in diameter, then transfer it to a piece of parchment paper.
  3. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Arrange the slices of fresh mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
  5. Scatter fresh basil leaves over the cheese. Drizzle a little extra virgin olive oil over the top and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Transfer the pizza (with the parchment paper) to the preheated pizza stone or baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  7. Remove from the oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then cut into slices. Serve immediately.
