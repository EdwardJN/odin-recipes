Classic Bruschetta

Classic Bruschetta is a quintessential Italian appetizer that combines the simple yet irresistible flavors of ripe tomatoes, fresh basil, garlic, extra virgin olive oil, and balsamic vinegar atop toasted slices of crusty bread. This dish celebrates the freshness of its ingredients, offering a burst of vibrant flavors and textures with each bite. It's the perfect starter for any meal, embodying the essence of Italian cuisine with its emphasis on quality and simplicity.

The key to a perfect bruschetta lies in the quality of the tomatoes and the bread. Juicy, ripe tomatoes and a loaf of artisanal bread, such as ciabatta or baguette, are essential. The tomatoes are tossed with basil, garlic, and olive oil, then spooned over grilled or toasted bread that's been rubbed with a garlic clove for an extra layer of flavor. A drizzle of balsamic reduction adds a sweet and tangy finish that makes this dish truly unforgettable.

Ingredients:

  • 4 large ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 baguette or ciabatta bread, sliced
  • Additional garlic cloves for rubbing the bread
  • Additional extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Steps:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, chopped basil, minced garlic, tablespoon of extra virgin olive oil, and balsamic vinegar. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Let the mixture sit for 15-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  2. Preheat your grill or broiler. Brush the bread slices with olive oil on both sides and toast them until they are golden brown and crispy, about 1-2 minutes per side.
  3. Once the bread is toasted, rub one side of each slice with a garlic clove for an extra kick of flavor.
  4. Spoon the tomato mixture generously over the garlic-rubbed side of each bread slice. Drizzle with more extra virgin olive oil and a few drops of balsamic reduction, if desired.
  5. Serve immediately, garnished with additional fresh basil leaves if you like.
